WHO WE ARE.

The professionals at Applied Building Sciences (ABS) know that a building is more than a structure; it’s a working system. An integration of many factors—engineering, architecture, materials, climate and environmental factors, and yes, the building’s users—all coalescing under one roof. Likewise, as a fully integrated company with cross-disciplinary expertise, we provide comprehensive building and construction services for clients across the Southeast and nationwide.

We are architects, engineers, floodplain managers and civil infrastructure consultants, among other experts, offering a full range of services for existing or new construction.

Reliable, trusted and exacting, ABS is the Lowcountry's first choice for construction-related needs.
